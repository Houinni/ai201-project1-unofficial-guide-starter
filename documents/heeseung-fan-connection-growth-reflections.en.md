# Heeseung Interview And Message Excerpts: ENGENE, Growth, And Self

## 221126

Q: I heard Heeseung's MBTI changed from I to E. What kind of change in mindset was that?

🦌: I thought about why I am so serious when I talk with people. It seems like I used to be the type who felt bothered in too many relationships, and I was not very active about things outside what I cared about. But recently, it seems I have not been like that as often.

🦌: I think something changed when we held concerts. Talking with everyone about all kinds of things became fun, and compared with before, I do not feel as shy around strangers. Introverted people do sometimes admire extroverted personalities, right? I used to feel a little sad because of that, but recently it does not seem to happen as much. It feels like my personality has changed. Even when preparing things at the company, preparing while talking with others is very different from preparing alone. Setting work aside, I think dialogue and communication let me be influenced by other people, and little by little that helps me improve myself. I used to be a very emotional person, but now I think I have become a little more balanced.

Q: It seems concerts had a big influence.

🦌: Yes. Even though it was the first time, it felt both familiar and unfamiliar, which was really fascinating. After the performance ended, I felt like my emotions and reason had separated. I felt relieved and happy, but there were still some regrets left. That was a little strange. I thought I would only feel good after it ended, but I feel the first concert gave me a lot of experience.
