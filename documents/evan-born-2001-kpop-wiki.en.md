## Career Timeline

- On June 1, 2020, he was introduced as a contestant on CJ ENM and Big Hit Entertainment's survival show I-LAND under the name Heeseung.
- He was a trainee from Big Hit Entertainment, now known as Big Hit Music.
- On September 18, 2020, he ranked fifth by global votes and became a member of ENHYPEN.
- On November 30, 2020, he debuted in ENHYPEN with the group's first mini album, Border : Day One.
- On March 10, 2026, Belift Lab announced that Heeseung would depart from ENHYPEN after in-depth discussions with the group about future direction and goals.
- According to the page's summary of Belift Lab's statement, Heeseung had a distinct musical vision, and the agency decided to respect it.
- The page says ENHYPEN would continue as a six-member group while Heeseung prepared for a solo debut under Belift Lab.
- Sometime in March 2026, Belift Lab added Heeseung to its website as a soloist under his birth name, Lee Heeseung.
- In March 2026, he also hinted during a fan call that his solo music would be released soon.
- On April 8, 2026, he launched solo SNS accounts.
- At the same time, Belift Lab changed his website profile to Evan, signaling the new stage name.
- On May 11, 2026, Belift Lab announced that Evan was preparing for a June solo debut.
- On June 11, 2026, it was announced that his debut would be on June 22, 2026 with "Ride or Die".

## Trivia

- The page explains Evan as a stage name representing another dimension of his identity and the essence of his musical artistry.
- The page says the Evan persona is meant to share music shaped by his own unique color and emotional depth.
- In the first episode of I-LAND, he performed "Boss" by NCT U.
- The page lists his MBTI personality type as INFJ.
- Before I-LAND, he trained for three years.
- The page says he is currently roommates with Jay, Sunghoon, and NI-KI.
- On I-LAND, he received the highest individual mark given by the producers, scoring 93 for his "Fake Love" performance.
- If he had not become an idol, he would have been an English teacher.
- His favorite ramen is Shin Ramyun.
